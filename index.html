Research Projects

Detection of DDOS Attacks at IXP level

In this project, we monitor and detect DDoS attacks online at IXP level to enable earlier detection. We also measure real-time mitigations that are taken against this attack by analyzing BGP routing data.

This paper is under review

Fingerprinting Smart Home Devices

This project focuses on monitoring network traffic generated by Smart Home devices that are behind a hub. Next, machine learning approaches such as seq2seq and deep learning are used to detect patterns in the traffic to fingerprint devices that could potentially reveal user's behaviors.

This paper is under review

Characterization of Phishing Sites via Site Deconstruction and Clustering Techniques

An automated tool that interacts with phishing sites by generating and providing the requested information and uses clustering techniques on the collected information and URL related properties to detect multiple pages generated by same phihsing kits and derive features unique to these phishing kits.
